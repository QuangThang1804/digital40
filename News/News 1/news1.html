Are content creators competing with Artificial Intelligence?


The popularity of smartphones and high-speed internet connections has facilitated the rapid and widespread production and distribution of content. This not only shows their personality and interests but also helps them connect with the community, build their brand, and even earn income.

The instruments for Artificial Intelligence are likewise evolving more quickly than civilization. It is not difficult to come across content on digital platforms produced by ChatGPT. ChatGPT is a large language model chatbot developed by OpenAI, attracting the attention of the world's technology community and users with its impressive ability to create text, translate languages, and answer questions. Currently, there are many GPT chats produced to support people, and Gemini is one of them.

Gemini is an artificial intelligence chatbot released by Google. This chatbot launched in February 2023 based on the large language model of the same name and became a direct response to the rapid growth of ChatGPT. Gemini is considered a "heavyweight warrior" in the artificial intelligence technology race, with high expectations for its power to respond to queries on laptops and phones and a huge data platform from Google. Gemini is designed to integrate text, images, and many other types of data, making the tool's conversational capabilities more engaging and natural. For example, the strength of DeepMind's AlphaGo system allows Gemini to solve complex Go situations. Or the ability to systematize and model extended languages for the feedback process. The scope of information is expanded to many fields, jobs, professions, and multimedia entertainment. Along with that are the latest updated versions, so Gemini can become a reputable personal assistant for users to trust.

ChatGPT is seen as a very promising technology that publishers and content producers may use to “produce content”. Because of this, there are instances in which we rely too much on AI and give the impression that it can take the place of people in the creation of engaging content. This is a matter of concern for content creators when facing the development of artificial intelligence (AI), such as GPT Chat. As AI can generate content at high speed and volume, this has threatened the uniqueness and value of human-generated content. Therefore, the challenges posed are that content creators must be proactive and know how to take advantage of GPT Chat to serve themselves, not depend on it. Innovators must consider how to use AI responsibly, avoiding abuse or misuse.
